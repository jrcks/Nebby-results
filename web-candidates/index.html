Frequently asked questions

Where can I find a list of API and IAM controls to see if MEGA S4 is right for me?

To evaluate whether our object storage meets your specific requirements, you can explore our comprehensive documentation for MEGA S4, which includes details on API and IAM controls.

This documentation is available on our GitHub repository.

Is MEGA S4 compatible with third-party applications and integration partners?

Yes, our object storage is compatible with a number of third-party S3 clients, and we are continuing to expand our support, adding new partnerships to enhance compatibility. If there is a specific third-party client you would like us to support, get in touch and let us know.

For a full list of supported solutions and detailed setup instructions, please visit our Help Centre.

How does MEGA S4 5x egress work?

We recognise that users often need to download data multiple times within a month, whether for running applications, backup services, or development purposes. Our 5x egress policy is crafted to accommodate these needs with predictable and competitive pricing.

Under the Pro Flexi plan with object storage, the 5x egress policy allows you to download (or “egress”) up to five times the amount of data you store in your MEGA S4 account each month. This generous allowance helps ensure that your data access needs are met efficiently. Please note that fair usage terms apply.

For further details, please see the Additional Terms of Service for MEGA S4.

Tell me about the Pro Flexi plan that MEGA S4 is part of.

The Pro Flexi plan is MEGA’s most versatile plan, designed to cater to a wide range of user needs, including our object storage customers, starting with a flat rate of 3 TB.

This plan is ideal for users who require dynamic, scalable options and want the flexibility to manage data with added services that support privacy and security.

What else do I get with Pro Flexi?

Our object storage is built on our Pro Flexi plan, which is designed to accommodate dynamic needs. Alongside MEGA S4, the Pro Flexi plan provides access to MEGA’s full suite of products, including:

– Private and secure online meetings: Stay connected with secure, encrypted meetings.
– Secure calls and messaging: Communicate confidently with private messaging and secure calls with MEGA’s chat and meetings service.
– VPN access: Protect your online activity with our integrated VPN service.

This comprehensive package ensures that you have all the tools you need in one convenient plan.

Can I get MEGA S4 on other MEGA plans?

No, our object storage is exclusively available on the Pro Flexi plan. This plan is specifically designed to provide the dynamic storage and flexible data management capabilities that MEGA S4 enables.

Why should I trust MEGA with my object storage?

At MEGA, privacy and security are at the forefront of everything we do. We have been market leaders in secure encryption services for more than 10 years and have never had a data breach. Ever.

Our company was founded on the belief of privacy as a human right and today, millions of customers worldwide trust us with their most precious data. That’s everything from precious family photos to creative projects and everything in between.

As an industry innovator, we are transparent in our practices and continue to refine our services based on user needs. MEGA S4 inherits the same high standards, offering a reliable, user-friendly solution with competitive pricing to meet your cloud storage demands.

We encourage you to read through our S4 documentation and Additional Terms of Service for MEGA S4, visit the Help Centre or reach out to our support team with any further questions you have.
